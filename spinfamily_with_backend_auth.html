About SpinFamily Wellness
SpinFamily is a family wellness tool that ends decision fatigue by turning routine-building into a fun, gamified experience. Every spin creates a balanced evening plan—so you spend less time deciding and more time connecting.
Features
  • AI-powered evening curation that eliminates daily decision fatigue
  • Build your own wellness activities with real-time family-safe moderation
  • Personalized profiles for each family member

  • Sync wellness plans across all family devices

🚀 Wellness Quick Start

How SpinFamily Works

Your daily wellness routine, gamified

1
🎯 Spin the Wheel
Tap "SPIN YOUR WELLNESS" to instantly end the "what should we do tonight?" debate. The wheel randomly selects a balanced wellness bundle across Chores, Outdoor, Family, and Self Care.
2
👀 Review Your Plan
Your wellness plan appears with a timeline and AI insight—showing why this specific balance supports your family's routine goals. No more decision fatigue.
3
🔄 Swap Activities
Not feeling a suggestion? Tap "Swap" to reshuffle any activity while keeping your wellness balance intact. You stay in control without the mental load.
4
✅ Accept or Respin
Love the plan? Accept it to build your wellness streak. Want a different vibe? Respin for a fresh wellness bundle—zero decision fatigue, every time.
5
📝 Add Custom Activities
Go to "Activities" to build your own wellness library. Every entry is moderated to ensure it supports a healthy, balanced family routine.
6
☁️ Save Your Progress
Tap "Anonymous Mode" to create an account and sync your wellness streaks, routines, and custom activities across every device your family uses.
💡
Pro Tip: Tap a family member avatar before spinning to get wellness suggestions tailored to their needs. Balance looks different for everyone!
